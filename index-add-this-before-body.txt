Add this just above </body> in index.html, AFTER your normal script.js line:

<script src="clara-sync-bridge.js?v=clara-v53-real-sync-20260618"></script>

Also update your existing CSS/script/manifest lines so the phone cannot use the old cached copy:

<link rel="manifest" href="manifest.json?v=clara-v53-real-sync">
<link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css?v=clara-v53-real-sync">
<script defer src="script.js?v=clara-v53-real-sync"></script>

If your app registers a service worker, make sure it registers this new one:

navigator.serviceWorker.register("./sw.js?v=clara-v53-real-sync", { updateViaCache: "none" });
